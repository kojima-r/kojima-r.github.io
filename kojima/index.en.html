I am studying artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning technology to apply them to real-world problems. My interests include the integration of deep learning and modeling technologies such as time-series modeling, logic programming, and probabilistic modeling. While deep learning has high performance and scalability, the methodology (modeling technology) for designing background knowledge tailored to the application domain is not well established. From two perspectives: deep learning and modeling technologies, I am addressing various real-world problems like medical applications and developing new methods for making scientifically innovative discoveries.
